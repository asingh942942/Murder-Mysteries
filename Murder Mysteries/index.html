The World of Murder Mysteries

Why Mysteries?

It's not that other genres, topics, or subjects aren't as entertaining as murder mysteries, it's just that my inner mind finds the most comfort in the shoes and eyes of the detective. The fierce protagonist who embodies the same level of inquisitiveness as I do, never fails to bring me the joy of unraveling a rich, carefully crafted story. Much like this protagonist, I always yearn to learn something new, accept a challenge that seems too daunting, or simply explore what's already been discovered. Sometimes, this style of thinking carries me far farther than my initial goals, much like this website, which was just supposed to be another boring lab for my Full Stack Web Development class!
